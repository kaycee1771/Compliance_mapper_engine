Article 5: ICT Risk Management Framework
Financial entities must establish an ICT risk management framework covering identification, protection, detection, response and recovery.

Article 6: ICT Systems Resilience
Ensure that ICT systems are resilient and recoverable. Conduct business impact analysis and disaster recovery testing.

Article 7: Incident Reporting
Report major ICT-related incidents to the competent authority within the required timeframes.

Article 8: Digital Operational Resilience Testing
Conduct advanced resilience testing using threat-led penetration testing at least every three years.

Article 9: Third-Party Risk
Identify and assess ICT third-party dependencies and manage risks arising from outsourcing and cloud services.

Article 10: Governance and Responsibility
The management body of the entity shall be responsible for digital resilience and ensure compliance with DORA requirements.
