A.5.1: Policies for Information Security
Establish a set of policies that provide management direction and support for information security.

A.6.1: Organizational Roles and Responsibilities
Define security roles and allocate responsibilities. Assign accountability for information security.

A.8.2: Information Classification
Classify information based on sensitivity and apply appropriate protection levels.

A.9.1: Access Control Policy
Limit access to information and systems based on business and security requirements.

A.12.3: Backup
Implement backup procedures to ensure data availability and integrity. Test backups regularly.

A.16.1: Management of Information Security Incidents
Establish procedures to report, assess, and respond to security incidents in a timely manner.
