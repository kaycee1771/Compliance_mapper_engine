Article 20: Cybersecurity Risk Management
Essential and important entities shall identify and take appropriate technical and organizational measures to manage the risks posed to the security of network and information systems.

Article 21: Incident Handling
Entities shall be capable of preventing, detecting, responding to and recovering from incidents. They must notify relevant authorities of significant incidents.

Article 22: Business Continuity
Entities must ensure continuity of their services and supply chains during and after incidents, including the use of backup and recovery mechanisms.

Article 23: Supply Chain Security
Organizations shall assess risks associated with suppliers and service providers and adopt security measures across the supply chain.

Article 24: Policy and Governance
Entities must adopt a governance framework to manage cybersecurity, assign responsibilities and continuously improve based on assessments.
